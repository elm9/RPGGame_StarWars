RETURN TO GAME

Star Wars
Episode VI: Return of the Jedi
The RPG

A long time ago in a galaxy far far away...

An epic battle of good versus evil is occuring, in this battle you choose which character you play as.

You must then defeat the remaining characters to win the game. Select your character and opponent, each available character has different Health Points, Attack Power, and Counter Attack Power.

Once you select your character and opponent press Attack.

Each time your character attacks, their Attack Power will increase by its base Attack Power. Your opponent will hit you with a Counter Attack after each Attack you make.

No characters in the game can heal themselves. If your HP reaches 0 or less you lose the game.
