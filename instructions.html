RETURN TO GAME

Star Wars
Episode VI: Return of the Jedi
The RPG





A long time ago in a galaxy far far away...




An epic battle of good versus evil is about to occur, choose your character wisely.

You must defeat the remaining characters to win the game. Select your opponent to fight them individually.

Each character has different Health Points, Attack Power, and Counter Attack Power.

Once you select your character and opponent press Attack. Each time your character attacks, your Attack Power will increase by its base Attack Power.

Your opponent will hit you with a Counter Attack after each Attack you make.

No characters in the game can heal themselves. If your HP reaches 0 or less you lose the game.
